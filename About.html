About Us

Introduction To The Scope Selfie Museum!

At The Scope, we're not your typical museum – we're all about fun and creativity! Our goal is to give you the best time ever with our awesome selfie rooms.

Our team has worked really hard to create cool themed rooms that'll make your selfies pop! Whether you're in a jungle or on the moon, you'll feel like a superstar!

We're always trying new things to make your visit super exciting. And guess what? Our prices won't empty your wallet! So come on over to The Scope and let's make some awesome memories together!"
